Everyday tools

Available to everyone on their Dashboard.

  • Using Your Dashboard — a tour of the dashboard menu and home summary.
  • Saved Searches — save, re-run, share, and delete searches.
  • Favorites — collect and share profiles you care about.
  • Prayers and Updates — post updates and prayer requests.
  • Donations — read, filter, and export donations.

For organization administrators

Extra tools that appear for organization administrators.

  • Managing Your Organization — organization details, sharing link, and donation form.
  • Managing Worker Profiles — add, edit, invite, and remove worker profiles.
  • Ministry Review — request and submit periodic budgets and reports.
