Favorites

Favorites is your personal collection of missionary profiles you want to keep track of.

Adding a favorite

Anywhere you see a profile — in search results or on a profile page — click the heart icon in the upper right corner of the worker's picture to add it to your favorites. Click the heart again to remove it. Your saved profiles then appear on the Favorites page as a set of profile cards.

Sharing your favorites

At the top of the Favorites page is a Share your favorites link. Anyone you send it to can view the same collection of profiles — a simple way to share a shortlist of missionaries with friends, family, or a small group considering who to support.
