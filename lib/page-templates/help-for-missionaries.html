Help for Missionaries Raising Support

Our passion is helping missionaries reach full funding and stay there. Whether you're just getting started, or you need a boost, we want to help.

How do we do that? We work hard to connect you with people interested in supporting a ministry like yours.

Our service is completely free.

First you create a profile, providing all the details of you ministry, such as where you are located, what kind of job skills your using, areas of the world your work is making an impact, and more. Then people can search through all our missionaries for someone matching their own criteria. Maybe they find you! They can read about your ministry. If they are interested, they can message you anonymously and get to know you more. When they are ready, they click your donate button and we send them to your existing donation page. We don't take any part of the donation.
