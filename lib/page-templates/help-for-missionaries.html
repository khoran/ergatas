Raising Support?

Our passion is helping missionaries reach full funding and stay there. Whether you're just getting started, or you need a boost, we want to help.

How do we do that? We work hard to connect you with people interested in supporting a ministry like yours.

Our service is completely free.

How does it work?
  • Create your profile, including details like
    • Location
    • Skills Used
    • Sending Organization
    • Areas of the world your work is making an impact
    • Support Level
  • Users find a missionary (maybe you!) that meets their criteria
  • Users read about your ministry and send anonymous messages you to ask questions
  • When a user is ready to donate, clicking your donate button sends them to your existing donation page on your organizations website.
