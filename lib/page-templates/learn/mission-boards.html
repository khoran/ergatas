Ergatas for Mission Committees

Find Kingdom workers that fit your church's passions and strategy.

How does it work?
  1. Workers from different organizations create detailed profiles on Ergatas.
  2. We provide filters to help you search through workers to find those that fit your church's existing passions and strategies.
  3. Use in-app anonymous messaging to contact workers to learn more about them and their ministries.

Our service is free to use and free for our workers. We don't take any part of donations made.

Ergatas helps your church find workers it's passionate about!

We offer the unique ability to search for missionaries across organizations with specific criteria.
