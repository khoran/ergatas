Monthly Prayer Card Drawing

An Ergatas partner has generously offered to pay for prayer postcards to be sent out with Mission Messenger for one randomly chosen worker each month. You can learn more about these prayer cards at missionmessenger.com.

Each month a different worker on Ergatas will be chosen to have their prayer cards sent out for free, up to a maximum of 500 partners.

Any active Ergatas worker is elgible for this benifit. To make sure you're in the drawing, check the box "Consider me for the monthly prayer card drawing" in your profile form, on the "Personal" tab.
