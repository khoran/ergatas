Our passion is helping missionaries reach full funding and stay there.

For missionaries, Ergatas is a great alternative to GoFundMe and other fundraising sites. Not only do we not have any fees, but we work hard to connect you with people interested in supporting a ministry like yours.

How does it work?
  1. Create your profile, including details like
    • Location
    • Skills Used
    • Sending Organization
    • Areas of the world your work is making an impact
    • Support Level
  2. Users find a missionary (maybe you!) that they would love to support
  3. They can read about your ministry and get to know you through anonymous messages
  4. When they are ready to donate, they can use our quick checkout process and we transfer 100% of the funds (minus bank fees) to your agency, designated for your support.
