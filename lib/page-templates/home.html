FAQs

When was Ergatas started?
Ergatas was founded in 2020.

What is a Kingdom Worker?

A "Kingdom Worker", or missionary, is anyone who goes to where Christ is not known to make Him known.

How do I create a profile?

If your sending organization is already approved, you can start your free profile here. Otherwise, start by submitting an application for your sending organization.

Can I contact the Kingdom Workers?

Yes! We provide a way to communicate anonymously via email with any of our Kingdom Workers. On the workers profile page, click the "Contact" button to send them a message. We will create new email addresses for you to communicate with each other thereafter.

How do I search for a Kingdom Worker?

Head to our search page to get started! You can use the search box at the top of the page to search for keywords, or click "Additional Search Filters" to use any of our 13 different ways to search! You can also click on the map icon to search by location.

Is my donation tax deductible?

Yes, for donors in the United States. Ergatas is a 501 c(3) organization.

How can I stay updated with Ergatas?

You can find the subscribe form at the bottom of any page. Just fill in your name and email and hit "Subscribe"!

How do I support Ergatas?

Your support helps us keep this site going! You can make an online donation here.
