A free search engine for finding missionaries

For Those Searching

Search for a missionary based on whatever criteria interests you. Perhaps you would love to support a missionary working in a similar field as you, or you have heart for a particular country. With Ergatas, you can find missionaries from different organizations, all over the world!

When you are ready to make a donation, the donate link will take you to the missionary organization's donate page where you can safely donate to the missionary you have selected.

For Missionaries

Missionaries who are raising funds can create a profile on the site at no cost, describing their ministry, where they will be ministering, what job skills they will be applying, and lots more.

While Ergatas will not remove the need for one-on-one partnership development, it aims to make the process a two way street: in addition to missionaries seeking partners, Ergatas allows partners to seek and find missionaries.

"Ergatas" is a Greek word that means "worker".

"Then he said to his disciples, 'The harvest is plentiful but the workers are few. Ask the Lord of the harvest, therefore, to send out workers (ergatas) into his harvest field.' "

Mathew 9:37-38 (NIV)
