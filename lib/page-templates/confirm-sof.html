Keep Up To Date

To get the most out of your profile, you need to keep it up-to-date! Anytime your support level changes, be sure to mark that in your profile. We'll send you some reminders, but if 4 months pass with no updates we will stop showing your profile and notify you by email. You can re-activate it at anytime using the link in the email, we won't delete anything.

If it just so happens that nothing has changed over that time period , that's fine, just load your profile and then click save. That way we know you haven't forgotten about us!
