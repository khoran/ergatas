Keep Up To Date

To get the most out of your profile, you need to keep it up-to-date! Anytime your support level changes, be sure to mark that in your profile. If a month goes by with no updates, we'll send you a reminder email. But if no updates are made for 2 months, we will stop showing your profile, and notify you by email. You can re-activate it at anytime using the link in the email.

If it just so happens that nothing has changed over the month, that's fine, just load your profile and then click save. That way we know you haven't forgotten about us!
