Update Requirment

To get the most out of your profile, you need to keep it up-to-date. Anytime your support level changes, be sure to update it in your profile also. We'll send you some reminders, but if 8 months pass with no updates we will stop showing your profile and notify you by email. You can re-activate it at anytime by saving your profile or using the link in the email.

If nothing has changed over that time period, just open your profile and then click save. That way we know you haven't forgotten about us!
