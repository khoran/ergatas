Weekly Prayer

Would you like to pray for a worker on Ergatas each week? We can send you weekly reminders.

Notifications

This is a good method if you have installed Ergatas on your phone as an app, or you plan to leave it open in a browser tab. You will get a notice once a week with a link to that weeks's worker. You can read about their ministry and pray for them.

This method will not work on Mac devices unfortunately.

When you click the button below, you'll be asked to grant permission to receive notifications. We'll then send you one right away to make sure it's working. If you don't see it, let us know. This needs to be done on each device you want a notification sent to.

You're signed up!

Email

If email works best for you, fill in the form in the footer below and we'll send you a weekly email with a link to that week's worker. You can unsubscribe anytime.
