Daily Prayer

Would you like to pray for a worker on Ergatas each day? We can send you daily reminders.

Notifications

This is a good method if you have installed Ergatas on your phone as an app, or you plan to leave it open in a browser tab. You will get a notice once a day with a link to that day's worker. You can read about their ministry and pray for them.

This method will not work on Mac devices unfortunately.

When you click the button below, you'll be asked to grant permission to receive notifications. We'll then send you one right away to make sure it's working. If you don't see it, let us know.

Others Methods?

Is there another way you'd like to be reminded? Email, text message, or something else? Let us know!
