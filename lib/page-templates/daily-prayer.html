Missionary of the Day

Would you like to pray for a worker on Ergatas each day? We can send you reminders each day. Just sign up using one of the methods below.

Notifications

This is a good method if you have installed Ergatas on your phone as an app, or you plan to leave it open in a tab on your desktop. You will get a notice once a day with a link to that day's worker. You can read about their ministry and pray for them.

This method will not work on Mac devices unfortunately

Email

We can also send you an email each day with a link to a worker to pray for. Just fill in the form below. You can unsubscribe at any time using the link in the email.
